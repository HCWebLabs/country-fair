We've got answers! Your fair guide is here

Gates open at 5:00 PM each evening Monday through Saturday (July 14-19). Wednesday is special - we open at 11:30 AM for Senior Citizen Day! The fair closes at 11:00 PM most nights, with admission gates closing 1 hour before fair closing time.

General admission is $10 per night (cash only at gate) and includes parking. Kids 6 and under are ALWAYS free! Kids 15 and under get free admission on sponsored nights (Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, and Saturday). Season passes for all six nights are $40 - a great value!

Outside food and beverages are not permitted. We have fantastic food vendors offering all your fair favorites - funnel cakes, corn dogs, fried Oreos, BBQ, ice-cold lemonade, and much more at reasonable prices!

For everyone's safety, only clear bags and security-checked diaper bags are allowed into the fair. Small personal items should be in clear bags so security can easily verify contents at entry.

Only service animals are permitted at the fair. With the crowds, noise, livestock, and summer heat, the fairgrounds can be stressful for pets. Service animals are always welcome!

We offer Pay-One-Price unlimited ride armbands for $20 per night (does not include admission). This is the best way to enjoy all the carnival rides! Individual ride tickets may also be available at the midway.

No individual under the age of 17 will be admitted without a parent or guardian. This policy helps ensure a safe, family-friendly environment for all fairgoers.

Don't miss the Lowes Tows Truck Pull Dash for Cash on Thursday and Friday nights featuring 2WD, 4WD, and Outlaw divisions! Wednesday's Senior Citizen Day includes free ice cream. The Fairest of the Fair pageants run Monday and Tuesday. Plus, we have nightly cash giveaways sponsored by local businesses!
